What's your dream job?

My dream job is a job where I can work remotely and keep my own schedule. My previous job had us working remotely when Covid-19 hit for almost a year and a half and I absolutely loved it. The lack of commute alone was great, but it also allowed me to spend my lunch breaks with my wife, who has also been working from home.

The other thing I look for in a job is the ability to make enough money to pay off my student loans and eventually retire. I know it is a little crass talking about money, but unfortunately it's a way of life. A good work ethic alone won't pay the bills, unfortunately.

And lastly, I would like a job where I can engage with like-minded people and interact with interesting products on concepts. A field related to either my gaming or movie-watching hobbies would be ideal!
